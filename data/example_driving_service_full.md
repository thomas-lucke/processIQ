We are a driving service. We provide pick-ups and drop-offs for sick people or elderly,
we drive school children, and we do group tours to events and back.

Requests come in two ways. Phone requests take 10 minutes to handle, cost $8.50 per call,
and have a 5% error rate (misunderstandings, wrong details). Email requests take only
5 minutes to process and cost $4.25, but have a 12% error rate because emails sit in
the inbox for 1–2 hours before anyone opens them. Both are handled by 1 dispatcher.

After the request is received we enter the order into our system. That takes 5 minutes,
costs $3.00, has a 2% error rate, and requires 1 person.

Once a week the manager and vice manager plan the upcoming week — which driver goes where
and does what. Assigning all orders to drivers takes roughly 5 hours, costs $85 per
planning session, and has an 8% error rate (scheduling conflicts, missed orders).
2 staff members are involved.

During the week there are always last-minute adjustments: drivers calling in sick, route
changes, extra orders. This takes about 10 minutes per order, costs $12 to handle,
has a 15% error rate, and involves 1 dispatcher.

Before each trip the driver comes to the office and gets a briefing — route details,
passenger needs, special instructions. That takes 10 minutes, costs $6.50, has a 3%
error rate, and involves 1 dispatcher and the driver.

Then the driver fulfills the order. Duration varies widely by order type, but on average
across all order types (single rides, doctor appointments with waiting time, school runs,
group tours) it works out to roughly 2 hours per order. Average cost is $45 per order,
error rate is 7%, and we have 60 drivers handling this.

After the trip our accountant processes the order: logs it in the accounting system and
sends the invoice. This takes 10 minutes of active work but typically sits in the queue
for about 2 hours before the accountant gets to it. Cost is $15 per order, error rate
is 4%, 1 person involved.

Finally we wait for payment. No active time is needed if the client pays within 2 weeks.
If not, we send a payment reminder, which takes 5 minutes, costs $3.50, has a 1% error
rate, and involves 1 person from accounting.

We have 60 drivers and 4 office staff, including the manager and vice manager.
